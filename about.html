About This Project

Understanding the purpose, inspiration, and learning behind this financial planning website.

Project Purpose

This website was created as a Grade 12 Finance/Accounting capstone project. The goal is to help high school students understand the cost of university and improve financial planning skills.

What I Learned

Through this project, I learned how to design and build a multi-page website, use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript together, and present financial information in a clear and meaningful way.

Real-World Connection

University is a major financial decision. This project connects classroom learning to real-life financial planning, budgeting, and decision-making.

My Goal

To make financial planning for university easier to understand and more accessible for high school students preparing for post-secondary education.
