About This Project

A Grade 12 Finance/Accounting capstone project focused on university cost planning and financial literacy.

Why I Chose This Topic

My name is Shiloh Zheng, and I am a Grade 12 student completing this Finance and Accounting Capstone Project. In the future, I am interested in studying business at university, particularly in the areas of finance or accounting. Although I currently have limited knowledge in these fields, I wanted to learn more about the financial realities of higher education and begin exploring topics related to my future academic interests.

Building the Website

Through Computer Science 12, I learned the fundamentals of website development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This project provided an opportunity to apply those technical skills in a meaningful way. By creating this website, I was able to combine technology with financial research and develop a resource that presents university cost information in a clear and organized format.

Project Scope

This website focuses on a selection of Canadian universities because many students at our school plan to continue their education in Canada. The information presented here is intended to provide a general overview of tuition and living expenses rather than serve as a complete financial planning guide. The goal is to help students become more aware of potential university costs and encourage them to think about financial planning before making post-secondary decisions.

Final Reflection

This project was not designed to tell students where they should attend university or which program they should choose. Instead, it was created to increase awareness of the financial considerations involved in post-secondary education. Through researching tuition fees, comparing living expenses, and developing this website, I gained a better understanding of the importance of financial planning and the role it may play in my future studies in business, finance, or accounting.
